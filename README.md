## About The Project

Specifically designed for youth and junior archers, UpShot is a simple, powerful but fun mobile application to assist with archery tournament practice and increase engagement in training sessions.

As a niche sport, archers often practice alone, or around other archers of widely differing skill levels and experience. It is often the case that they only face archers of equal skill levels at large organised tournaments, which do not happen very often. In addition, Archery matches take place using a head to head, set scoring based system, where shooting happens asynchronously between two archers. The pressure of seeing your opponent shoot a high score, and knowing you must now shoot a perfect 10 to stay in the tournament, is very difficult to replicate in training sessions.

Until now!

This app replicates precisely the tournament and match play archery experience by allowing the user to select a specific opponent, with a unique difficulty level (to match their own skill and provide close matches). The user will then shoot head to head against this opponent with arrow scores given in real time, replicating the pressure, timing and flow of a real tournament, all in your pocket, on your iPhone!

With a fun visual design and responsive interface, the app also intends to increase participation and engagement amongst youth and junior archers in training programs.

## How it Was Built

The UpShot project was built by a single developer (Aaron Cardwell), over a 6 week period, using an agile development methodology focusing on 3 sprints of 2 weeks length each.

## Getting Started

To get a local copy of the app up and running and try it for yourself, follow these simple steps.

### Prerequisites

You will need:

* A Mac with MacOS
* XCode 12 or above

### Installation

Below is an example of how you can instruct your audience on installing and setting up your app. This template doesn't rely on any external dependencies or services.

1. Clone the repo to local directory.
   ```sh
	git clone https://github.com/AaronEC/UpShot.git
   ```
2. Open with XCode

3. Run in simulator for iPhone 13 Pro.

	**OR**

4. Build package and side load into a local iPhone running iOS 15 or higher.
